I am a penultimate-year Mechatronics Engineering undergraduate at the Federal University of Technology, Minna. I am an independent researcher with ML Collective and previously a participant in the Research Experience for Nigerian Undergraduates (RENEU) program.

Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant in the Jossou Research Group at MIT – Massachusetts Institute of Technology, mentored by DongHun Ryu. I also presented research at DSN AI Bootcamp 2025 – Data Science Nigeria.

In 2026, I will join the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems as a Research Intern, working on structured fine-tuning methods under Jonas Geiping and Antonio Orvieto.

My research focuses on the design, control, and learning of autonomous systems under resource constraints. I investigate parameter-efficient fine-tuning, model compression, knowledge distillation, and embedded machine learning to enable compute- and energy-efficient intelligent systems.
