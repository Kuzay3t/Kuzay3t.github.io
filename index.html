I am a penultimate-year Mechatronics Engineering undergraduate at the Federal University of Technology, Minna. I am an independent researcher with ML Collective and previously a research intern at the Jossou Research Group at MIT – Massachusetts Institute of Technology, mentored by DongHun Ryu, through the Research Experience for Nigerian Undergraduates (RENEU) program.

My research focuses on the design, control, and learning of autonomous systems under resource constraints. I investigate parameter-efficient fine-tuning, model compression, knowledge distillation, and embedded machine learning to enable compute- and energy-efficient intelligent systems.
